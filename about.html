About XINFENG LEWA

Bringing joy to children through quality playground equipment

Who We Are

XINFENG LEWA TOYS CO., LTD is a professional manufacturer specializing in the design, production, and export of children's outdoor playground equipment. Based in China, we have been serving clients worldwide for over 10 years, delivering durable, safe, and fun playground solutions to kindergartens, communities, amusement parks, and schools.

Our product range includes large combination slides, swings, seesaws, and spring rockers — all designed to stimulate children's physical development, social skills, and creativity in a safe outdoor environment.

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves a safe and fun place to play. Our mission is to make high-quality playground equipment accessible to communities around the world at factory-direct prices. We are committed to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction in every product we make.

Quality & Safety

All our products are manufactured using high-quality raw materials and undergo rigorous quality control. We comply with international safety standards to ensure every piece of equipment is safe for children. From material selection to finished product, every step is monitored and tested.
